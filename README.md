Festman Stores: Financial Analysis Dashboard
A comprehensive Power BI financial dashboard designed to analyze retail performance across global markets, segments, and product lines. This project focuses on comparing current year performance against prior year metrics to drive data-driven decision-making.

Core Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
Sales: 861,132 (+225.36% YoY growth)

Orders: 92,311,095 (+249.46% YoY growth)

Profit: 13,015,238 (+235.58% YoY growth)

Profit %: 14.10% (slight decrease of 3.97% from previous year)

COGS: 79,295,857 (+251.85% YoY growth)

Key Visualizations & Features
Geographic Performance: * Orders by Country: Bar chart tracking volume across Canada, France, USA, Mexico, and Germany.

Profit Margin by Country: Comparative analysis showing Germany leading with 15.7%.

Segment Analysis:

Profit Margin by Segment: Identifies Channel Partners as the highest margin (73.1%) and Enterprise as a loss-making segment (-3.1%).

Detailed Product Matrix: A granular table breaking down margins by segment and specific product lines (Amarilla, Carretera, etc.) using status indicators.

Sales Trends:

Time Series: Line chart visualizing sales fluctuations by month throughout the fiscal year.

Product Performance: Rankings of top products by sales amount, led by "Paseo."

Discounting Strategy:

Donut Chart: Analyzes sales distribution across High, Medium, Low, and No discount bands to monitor margin erosion.

Technical Implementation
Tool: Power BI / Excel

Data Modeling: Implementation of a Date Table for Year-over-Year (YoY) Time Intelligence calculations.

DAX Measures: Custom calculations for Profit Margin %, YoY growth variances, and conditional formatting flags.

UI/UX: High-contrast dark theme optimized for executive presentation with synchronized filtering.
